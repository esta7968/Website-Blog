Northern Delights

Welcome to Nigeria's intriguing northern region, where old traditions combine harmoniously with modern life. Northern Nigeria is a fascinating fabric of culture, history, and hospitality, with bustling towns and quiet rural settings.

Katsina:

A glimpse into Nigeria's heritage. Katsina, located in the heart of Northern Nigeria, entices visitors with its rich history and dynamic current. Katsina, one of Nigeria's oldest towns, is home to several historical structures, including the well-known Kusugu Well and the massive Umaru Musa Yar'adua Central Mosque. Immerse yourself in the rich Hausa culture by wandering around crowded marketplaces, sampling native cuisine and admiring beautiful handicrafts.

Niger:

Where Nature Meets Tradition. Visit Niger State and explore a place of natural wonders and cultural riches. From the breathtaking Gurara Falls to the historic Kainji National Park, Niger has limitless options for adventure and discovery. Dive into the Nupe people's traditions, which include colourful festivals and intriguing music and dance performances. Don't pass up the opportunity to enjoy wonderful Nupe food, a beautiful combination of flavours and spices.
