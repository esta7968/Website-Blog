About Me

Hello, fellow travel lover! I am Esther Arowolo, a patriotic Nigerian from the lovely state of Osun. Nigeria's vast diversity and cultural tapestry have attracted me since I was a child, and my wanderlust has taken me on innumerable journeys around this beautiful country.

Exploring Nigeria’s Diversity

I've had the opportunity to travel across Nigeria, from the busy streets of Abuja, our dynamic capital city, to the tranquil landscapes of Plateau State. Along the trip, I engaged myself in each region's distinct cultures and customs, from the northern regions of Katsina and Niger to the southern gems of Osun and Oyo.

My Journey

With each tour, I discovered hidden treasures and unique experiences. Whether walking through the rocky beauty of Buchi, taking in the natural beauties of Kwara, or learning about Gombe's rich history, each trip has left an unforgettable impact on my spirit.

Accepting the Spirit of Adventure

My travel attitude is simple: accept the unknown and seek out new experiences. From remote villages to bustling cities, I like the opportunity to uncover each destination's heart and spirit. Whether it's eating local foods, engaging with locals, or going on adrenaline-pumping experiences, I'm always eager for the next interesting adventure.

Sharing My Adventures

I started this travel blog to express my enthusiasm for exploration and to inspire others to go on their own journeys. Through colourful storytelling, practical suggestions, and breathtaking pictures, I aim to take you to Nigeria's various landscapes and vibrant cultures.
